ANAESTHESIA

ANAESTHESIA

The Department of Anaesthesia at Doctors Hospital Lahore provides state-of-the-art care for patients undergoing a variety of surgical procedures. We provide complete, advanced anesthesiology services for all surgical procedures performed at Lahore, ranging from minor outpatient procedures to major operations such as transplantation.
The anaesthetic department provides expertise in a wide variety of surgical specialty disciplines including Cardiac, Thoracic-Foregut, Neurosurgery, ENT, Vascular, Bariatric, Colorectal, Urology, Gynaecology, Oncology, Ophthalmology, Orthopaedics and Sports Medicine.

Services

  • Plan peri-operative care and explain proposed anesthetic techniques.
  • Post-operative pain management.
  • Painless labour (Epidural).
  • Anaesthesia for paediatrics and neonates.
  • Anaesthesia outside the OT (E.g. Paediatric patients undergoing diagnostic MRI)
  • Ambulatory Anaesthesia.
