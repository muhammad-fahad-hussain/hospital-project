What is Opsoclonus-Myoclonus Syndrome(OMS)?

OMS is a rare neuroimmunological disorder in children and adults. Symptoms include rapid multidirectional conjugate eye movements, myoclonus, tremors, encephalopathy, and cerebellar ataxia. Behavioral changes in adults and irritability in children are also common.
