About Doctor Hospitals

About Doctor Hospitals Group

Doctors Hospitals Group is a medical complex in Pakistan that was founded with the goal of providing specialized services to patients in the areas of orthopedics and traumatology. The idea behind the group was to provide professional consultations and treatments to patients without the need for them to travel abroad, particularly those in the Punjab province and surrounding areas.

One of the key benefits of the Doctors Hospitals Group is that patients no longer need to travel abroad for medical treatment. This not only saves them the cost and inconvenience of travel, but it also ensures that they can receive the highest quality care possible without the need for language translation or cultural barriers.

The Doctors Hospitals Group has a strong commitment to patient care, and this is reflected in its mission to provide quality healthcare in all specialties with the highest level of ethical standards and personalized care. It has a patient-centric approach, which means that patients are at the center of everything it does.
Mission of the Group is ‘To provide quality healthcare in all specialties with highest level of ethical standards and personalized care to achieve superior medical outcome and patient satisfaction.

DH Group said that the Group is inspired by the verse from the Holy Quran:

ومن احياها فكأنما احيا الناس جميعا“ المائد 32”
“And if anyone saved a life, it would be as if he saved the life of all mankind.” Al-Maidah (32)
